RS 232 is a form of serial data transmission. 

### Hardware Interfaces

1. 9 pin DB9 cable

### Uses in PLCs
To communicate with other modules or PLCs.

### Types of RS 232 Devices

1. DTE eg. a computer
2. DCE eg. a modem

This is because DTE to DTE and DCE to DCE communication is limited.
